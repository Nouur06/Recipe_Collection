
#Appetizers

##Stuffed Mushrooms

**Ingredients:**Mushrooms,cream cheese,garlic,parsley,breadcrumbs

**Instructions:**Remove mushroom stems, fill with cream cheese mixture, bake at 190C for 20 mins
**Cooking Time:**25 mins
##Chicken Wings
**Ingredients:**Chicken wings, hot sauce, butter, garlic powder, salt
**Instructions:**Season wings, bake at 200C for 40 mins, toss in hot sauce and butter
**Cooking Time:**45 mins
