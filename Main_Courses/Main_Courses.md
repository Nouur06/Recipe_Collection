## Beef Tacos

 ## Salmon with Rice

**Ingredients:**Salmon fillet, white rice, soy sauce, ginger, garlic

**Instructions:**Cook rice, pan fry salmon 4 mins each side, drizzle soy sauce

**Cooking Time:**35 mins
