# Desserts
## Tiramisu
**Ingredients:**Mascarpone cheese, espresso, ladyfingers, eggs, sugar, cocoa powder
**Instructions:**Dip ladyfingers in espresso, layer with mascarpone cream, dust with cocoa powder, refrigerate overnight
**Cooking Time:**30 mins + overnight chill
## Banana Pudding
**Ingredients:**Bananas,vanilla pudding mix, milk, whipped cream, vanilla wafers
**Instructions:**Make pudding with milk, layer with wafers and banana slices, top with whipped cream, chill for 2 hours
**Cooking Time:**15 mins + 2 hours chill
